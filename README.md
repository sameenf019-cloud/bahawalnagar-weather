# 🌤️ Bahawalnagar Weather App

Live weather, AI farming advice, flood alerts & Urdu summary for Bahawalnagar, Punjab, Pakistan.

[![Open in Colab](https://colab.research.google.com/assets/colab-badge.svg)](https://colab.research.google.com/github/sameenf019-cloud/linkedin-post-generator/blob/main/weather_app.ipynb)

[![Hugging Face](https://img.shields.io/badge/🤗%20Hugging%20Face-Live%20Demo-yellow)](https://huggingface.co/spaces/Sameenfatimaa/bahawalnagar-weather)

## Features
- 🌡️ Live weather & 7-day forecast
- 🌾 Crop calendar (wheat & cotton)
- 💧 Smart irrigation advisor
- 🐛 Pest & disease risk
- 🌊 Flood & monsoon tracker
- ⚠️ Heat & dust storm alerts
- 🏫 School day safety checker
- 🐄 Livestock heat stress (THI)
- 🌬️ Air quality index
- 🇵🇰 Weekly Urdu digest
- 🤖 AI advisor (Urdu/English)
